MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT
relating to the supply and implementation of a retail inventory and EPOS platform
Date:	14 March 2024
Parties:	
(1)  MERIDIAN RETAIL GROUP PLC (company no. 03992145) of Meridian House, 40 Kingsway, London WC2B 6EX ("the Customer"); and
(2)  TECHFLOW SOLUTIONS LIMITED (company no. 07781230) of Optimum Park, 12 Brindley Way, Reading RG1 8DF ("the Supplier").
1.  Definitions and scope
1.1  The Supplier shall design, build, configure, test and implement the cloud-based inventory management and EPOS platform ("the Platform") in accordance with the Statement of Work set out at Schedule 1 (the "SOW").
1.2  The Supplier shall perform the Services with reasonable skill and care and in accordance with good industry practice.
2.  Charges
2.1  The total charges for the Services are £2,400,000 (excluding VAT), payable against the milestones set out in the SOW and as may be varied by agreement in writing.
3.  Timetable and change control
3.1  The Supplier shall use reasonable endeavours to achieve the target go-live date stated in the SOW. The parties acknowledge that the implementation timetable is a planning estimate and may be adjusted through the change control procedure.
3.2  Any change to the scope, deliverables or timetable shall be agreed in writing by both parties by means of a signed Change Order. A signed Change Order varies this Agreement to the extent stated in it.
