DEED OF VARIATION No. 1
to the Master Services Agreement dated 14 March 2024
Date:	28 June 2024
PARTIES: (1) Meridian Retail Group plc; and (2) TechFlow Solutions Limited.
Recitals
(A)  The parties entered into the Master Services Agreement dated 14 March 2024 ("the MSA").
(B)  The parties wish to vary the payment milestone profile to reflect cash-flow phasing. This Deed does not vary the scope of the works or the target go-live date.
Operative provisions
1.  With effect from the date of this Deed, clause 4.1 of the SOW is varied so that the charges are payable: 20% on M1, 20% on M2, 30% on M3 and 30% on M4.
2.  Save as varied by this Deed, the MSA remains in full force and effect.

EXECUTED as a DEED by the parties on the date first above written.
