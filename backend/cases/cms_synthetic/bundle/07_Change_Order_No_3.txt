1.  Description of change
1.1  At the Customer’s request, the Supplier shall design, build and implement a customer loyalty module ("the Loyalty Module"), comprising points accrual, redemption at EPOS, and a loyalty member database. The Loyalty Module was not part of the original scope under SOW-01.
2.  Effect on timetable
2.1  The parties agree that, to accommodate the additional works, the go-live date is revised from 1 October 2024 to 18 November 2024. The revised date applies to the Platform as a whole.
3.  Effect on charges
3.1  The additional charge for the Loyalty Module is £180,000, payable on go-live.
