December trading — a difficult picture

Helena,
A heads-up before the board call. December trading is well below plan and there are a few moving parts:
1) The Lutterworth distribution centre flooded on 3 December and was effectively out of action for around ten days. Roughly 40 stores had gaps on key lines through the first half of the month as a result. This is unrelated to the platform.
2) The market is soft — footfall is down across the sector and several competitors have gone early and deep on discounting. Our like-for-likes were already tracking behind in October.
3) On top of that, the platform stock-sync problems haven’t helped at the tills, though the teams have largely been working around them with manual checks.
I’d be wary of attributing the whole shortfall to the platform when the DC flood and the wider market are doing a lot of the damage. Happy to talk it through before the call.
