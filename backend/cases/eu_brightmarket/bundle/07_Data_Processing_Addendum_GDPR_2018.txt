DATA PROCESSING ADDENDUM (GDPR, 2018)

Data Processing Addendum, dated 20 May 2018, amending the 2017 Data Processing Agreement.

The parties record that the General Data Protection Regulation (Regulation (EU) 2016/679) applies from 25 May 2018.

All references in the 2017 Agreement to Directive 95/46/EC are replaced: that Directive was repealed by Article 94 GDPR, and international transfers are henceforth governed by Chapter V of the GDPR and the Commission's standard contractual clauses.
