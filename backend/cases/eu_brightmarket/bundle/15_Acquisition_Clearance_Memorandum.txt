ACQUISITION CLEARANCE MEMORANDUM

Acquisition Clearance Memorandum — Cobalt acquisition of NorthStar Analytics, 12 September 2024.

The combined EU turnover of the parties fell below the thresholds of the EU Merger Regulation; the transaction was nonetheless notified to, and cleared by, the competent authority on 9 September 2024.

The transaction was assessed under Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 (the EU Merger Regulation); interoperability commitments were given and recorded.
