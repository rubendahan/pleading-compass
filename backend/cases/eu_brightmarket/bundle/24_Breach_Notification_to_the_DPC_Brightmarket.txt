BREACH NOTIFICATION TO THE DPC (BRIGHTMARKET)

Personal-data breach notification by Brightmarket Retail GmbH (controller) to the Data Protection Commission, submitted 16 October 2025.

We became aware of the breach via our processor's notification on 11 October 2025 and are notifying your office on 16 October 2025; we recognise this is beyond the 72-hour period and explain the delay below.

On our current assessment the breach affected approximately 19,000 data subjects whose records were confirmed to have been accessed; investigation continues.
