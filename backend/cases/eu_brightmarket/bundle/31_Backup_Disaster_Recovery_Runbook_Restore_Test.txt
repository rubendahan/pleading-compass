BACKUP & DISASTER-RECOVERY RUNBOOK + RESTORE TEST

Backup & Disaster-Recovery Runbook and Restore-Test Record, 15 July 2025.

Backups: hourly snapshots of the production data stores are taken and retained for 30 days, with daily off-region copies.

Restore test of 15 July 2025: a full restore from snapshot recovered 99.4% of the test dataset within the four-hour recovery-time objective.

The 0.6% not recovered relates to a known indexing gap in a legacy table flagged for decommissioning; it is unrelated to any specific incident.
