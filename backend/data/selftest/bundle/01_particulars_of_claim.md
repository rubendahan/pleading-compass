The Claimants are subpostmasters who operated branch Post Offices using the Horizon accounting system supplied and maintained by the Defendant.

The Horizon system contained bugs, errors and defects which were capable of generating apparent shortfalls in branch accounts that did not reflect any actual loss of cash or stock.

At all material times the Defendant, acting by its agent Fujitsu, had the ability to access and alter branch transaction data remotely, without the knowledge or consent of the subpostmaster.

The Defendant's senior management knew, or ought to have known, of the said defects in Horizon, and nonetheless represented to subpostmasters that the system was accurate and reliable.
