The Defendant denies each of the allegations set out in the Particulars of Claim save as expressly admitted below.

The Horizon system was at all material times accurate and robust, and any shortfalls recorded in branch accounts were caused by the error or dishonesty of the subpostmaster concerned.

The Defendant had no ability to alter branch transaction data remotely, and did not at any time do so.
