type: witness
party: claimant
date: 2018-09-03

I operated the branch Post Office at Dalmellington between 2010 and 2015 and used the Horizon terminal daily to balance the branch accounts.

On numerous occasions Horizon recorded unexplained shortfalls at the point of balancing, sometimes of several thousand pounds, which appeared overnight without any corresponding transaction.

No cash was ever in fact missing from my branch; the discrepancies existed only on the Horizon screen and could not be reconciled against the cash and stock physically present.

When I telephoned the helpline I was told that I was the only subpostmaster experiencing the problem and that I remained liable to make good the shortfall.
