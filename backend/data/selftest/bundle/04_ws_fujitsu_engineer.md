I was employed by Fujitsu at the Software Support Centre responsible for the Horizon system during the relevant period.

Support staff at the Centre were able to insert and edit balancing transactions directly into a branch account remotely, in order to correct discrepancies identified in the data.

Such remote corrections were on occasion carried out without notifying the subpostmaster at the branch concerned.
