type: expert
party: neutral
date: 2019-01-20

I was instructed to examine the reliability of the Horizon system and the mechanisms by which apparent shortfalls could arise.

I identified a defect known as the Dalmellington bug, by which a screen freeze could cause a single transaction to be recorded multiple times, inflating the apparent balance.

I further identified the Receipts and Payments Mismatch bug, which produced apparent shortfalls in branch accounts that did not correspond to any real cash difference.
